About: Debris üî•

Debris is a Google Maps-based interface that allows you to report the presence and location of debris and detritus leftover from a natural disaster. You can also find and respond to and resolve these issues on the map. Debris is meant to be an intuitive experience to aid people who wish to participate in natural disaster cleanup or report such issues.

Debris is a collaborative project for EECS 330: Human Computer Interaction. The collaborators are Naomi Chan, Samuel Naser, Michael Guo, and Alex Zhu, who are students at Northwestern University.
